Exact locations that capture the most network revenue when 142 stores consolidate into kiosks. Ranked by capture; each spot is a proven existing micro-location. Not tied to malls/stations.

Kiosks
Placement maximizes retained revenue: online kept within ~5km (delivery), offline decays fast with distance from the kiosk (0.3km→85%, 1.5km→40%, 4km→8%). Dots = existing stores, colored by which kiosk they fold into; big numbers = kiosk spots. Even optimally placed, consolidation retains under half of current sales — the rest is offline that can't travel. Growth must come from net-new footfall + menu uplift on top, which this retained view excludes. No pilot yet = retention is modeled, not measured.
